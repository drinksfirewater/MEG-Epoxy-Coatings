Pricing

Transparent Pricing for Real-World Floors.

Most residential flake garage systems start around $5.00 per sq ft, with final pricing based on concrete condition, system type, and any repairs or moisture mitigation needed.

Every slab is a little different. We’ll walk you through why your floor costs what it costs—no surprises.

How We Build Your Price

We look at square footage, current concrete condition, cracking or spalling, moisture, and the system you’re after (flake, metallic, quartz, stain & seal, etc.).
From there we give you clear, written pricing and explain any add-ons like heavy repair, moisture vapor barriers, or upgraded topcoats.

Add-Ons & Upgrades

What Else Might Be On Your Quote?

These items don’t apply to every floor, but when they do, we’ll show them clearly in your estimate so you can see exactly what you’re paying for.

Moisture
Moisture Vapor Barrier (MVB)

For slabs with measurable moisture issues that can cause bubbling or peeling, we may recommend an MVB system under your coating.

  • Installed as a separate epoxy layer
  • Helps protect adhesion long-term
  • More common at ground level or older slabs
Repair
Heavy Crack / Spall Repair

Beyond simple hairline cracks, deeper cracks, spalling, or damaged areas may need extra prep and repair materials.

  • Structural crack chasing and filling
  • Rebuilding pits and spalled areas
  • Leveling low or uneven spots
System Upgrades
Metallic, Quartz & High-Build

Metallic, quartz, and certain commercial builds require more material and labor than a standard flake garage system.

  • Metallic epoxy showroom floors
  • Quartz broadcast for high traction
  • High-build systems for demanding use
