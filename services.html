Floor Systems

Epoxy, Flake, Metallic & Quartz Systems

High-performance, resin-based systems designed to handle hot tires, chemicals, foot traffic, and equipment—while still looking clean and professional.

Flake Systems
Full Broadcast Flake Epoxy

Our most popular system for garages, home shops, and detail bays. Epoxy base, full flake broadcast, and a durable clear topcoat.

  • Helps hide dirt, dust, and wear
  • Great traction and light reflection
  • Wide range of flake color blends
The go-to choice for clean, bright garages.

Pigmented Epoxy Floors

Solid color epoxy systems for a clean, uniform look in garages, workspaces, and commercial areas where a simple, professional finish is key.

  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Great for shops, warehouses, and utility spaces
  • Color options to match your space or brand
Minimal, professional, and functional.

Metallic Epoxy Floors

High-end, showroom-style finishes using metallic pigments for a deep, 3D look. Ideal for feature areas, showrooms, and custom spaces.

Quartz
Quartz Broadcast Systems

Broadcast quartz systems for extra traction, durability, and chemical resistance in wet or high-demand environments.

  • Excellent slip resistance
  • Great for ramps, wash bays, and busy work zones
  • Color blends for a clean, professional look
Built for demanding, high-traffic areas.
Topcoats
Polyaspartic Topcoat Systems

UV-stable, fast-curing polyaspartic topcoats installed over epoxy, flake, or quartz systems for extra durability and quick turnaround.

  • Faster return-to-service than standard epoxies
  • Improved scratch and chemical resistance
  • Excellent UV stability for sun-exposed areas
Finish your floor strong and fast.
Prep & Repair

Concrete Grinding, Crack Repair & Moisture Control

Long-lasting floors start with solid prep. We don’t coat over problems—we fix them so your coating can actually bond and perform.

Prep
Diamond Grinding

Mechanical surface prep that opens the pores of the concrete, removes weak or contaminated layers, and creates the proper profile for coatings.

  • High-bond surface profile for epoxy & polyaspartic
  • Removal of old coatings, paints, and glue in many cases
  • Dust-controlled equipment for cleaner job sites
Repair
Crack & Spall Repair

We chase and fill cracks, rebuild small pits and spalls, and level out problem areas so your floor looks and wears better.

  • Crack chasing and structural fill
  • Pit and spall patching with repair mortars
  • Blends into surrounding concrete under coatings
Moisture
Moisture Vapor Barrier (MVB)

For slabs with moisture issues that can cause coatings to bubble or peel, we can install a dedicated moisture mitigation system.

  • Moisture readings when necessary
  • Industrial-grade MVB epoxies
  • Helps protect adhesion long-term
Important for problem slabs and ground-level spaces.
Stain & Seal

Concrete Staining & Clear Sealing

Want to enhance the look of your concrete without a full build coating system? Stain and seal options can give color, protection, and easier cleaning.

Stain
Acid Stain Systems

Chemical-reactive stains that create variegated, stone-like color in the concrete surface itself, followed by neutralization and sealing.

  • Multi-step staining process
  • Neutralizing and rinsing between coats
  • Choice of sealer sheen (gloss or matte)
Sealer
Clear Concrete Sealing

Keep your slab from dusting, make cleanup easier, and add light gloss or protection to bare concrete in shops, patios, and utility spaces.

  • Dust control and stain resistance
  • Good for patios, shops, and storage areas
  • Slip-resistant additives available if needed
Safety & Customization

Non-Slip Additives & Custom Flake Blends

Dial in the traction and look of your floor with safety additives and custom color options.

Traction
Aluminum Oxide Non-Slip Additives

Fine or aggressive texture options broadcast into the coating system to help with grip in wet or high-traffic zones.

  • Great for ramps, entries, and work zones
  • Can be tuned lighter or more aggressive
  • Works with epoxy, polyaspartic, and sealers
Colors
Custom Flake Color Blends

Match your garage theme, company branding, team colors, or just personal style with custom flake blends.

  • Brighten dark spaces
  • Helps hide dirt and debris
  • Brand-friendly color combinations
Style and function in one system.
