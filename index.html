What to Expect During Your Project

1. On-site quote: We measure your space, inspect the concrete, and talk about color and flake options.
2. Prep day: Grinding, repairs, and moisture checks (if needed).
3. Install: Primer, epoxy/flake broadcast, and topcoat for a durable, cleanable surface.
4. Cure: Light foot traffic typically in 24 hours; vehicle traffic as instructed.

You’ll know exactly what system you’re getting, what it costs, and how to care for it.
