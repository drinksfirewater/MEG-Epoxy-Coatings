Prep & Repair Services

We don’t coat over problems—we fix them first.

Prep

Diamond Grinding

Mechanical surface prep that opens concrete pores and removes weak layers.

  • High-bond grinding profile
  • Coating/glue/paint removal
  • Dust-controlled equipment
Repair

Crack & Spall Repair

We chase and fill cracks, repair small pits, and ensure a smooth base.

  • Structural crack repair
  • Pit/spall patching
  • Stronger final finish
